UnLink: A Case Study

What is UnLink?

A social media platform for non-social media users. The only information allowed for posting relates to opportunities and events that are being promoted exclusively on social media: this includes giveaways, contests, promotions, sales, concerts, pop-ups, etc.

Those disconnected from social media don't have access to the resources and opportunities that social media users have. Unlink attempts to solve that problem, filtering out the unnecessary friend, family, and influencer content that inevitably clogs up social media feeds.

UnLink provides users with opportunities that would only be available through social media without the distractions of traditional social media applications. Businesses and organizations promoting through UnLink benefit from increased visibility and recognition in offline communities.

What’s So Great About UnLink?

Other sites offering giveaways and coupons are often outdated or ad-heavy. UnLink is different: it filters and organizes real social media opportunities for users who don’t want to join social platforms.

It minimizes distraction and promotes community connection while helping businesses find new audiences.

Incorporated Design Patterns

  • Onboarding: Introduces users, sets location, and helps personalize app usage.
  • Navigation Menu: Enables switching between 5 screens and organizing content.
  • Feedback: Animations add surprise when users engage with content.
  • Drop Down Menu: Modifies user filters on onboarding/home screens.
  • Location Page: Optional location setup depending on user preferences.
